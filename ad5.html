W.H.O. WILL SUPPORT TRADITIONAL/AYURVEDA SYSTEM OF MEDICINES


W.H.O. has started a special program to support and regularize traditional system of medicines. Under this program,WHO will collect data and differentiation from every Country regarding traditional system of medicine,and will prepare an official data. South east asia,Africa,latin America,and almost all part of world,at the primary health System is dependent on traditional system of medicine. In Europe and America,people are getting attracted towards yoga,pryanam,and tradional system of medicines,specially herbal based medicines,because of side effect of Allopathic medicines. According to WHO many countries have collected data regarding this,and done the clinical research.WHO will start its process from china,korea,and japan.It is assumed that Indian system of medicine will get benefited by this program. Source: Nav Bharat times 17/12/10
