YOGA AND AYURVEDA WILL BE INCLUDED IN AMERICAN CANCER RESEARCH


HOUSTEN: There will be a MOU between INDIA and AMERICA currentely in future under which in research of prevention and cure of cancer in AMERICA,PATANJALI YOG PEETHS yog and ayurvedic treatment procedures will be included. PATANJALI YOG PEETH's spokesperson S.KTijarewal said that AMERICA'S renowned ONCOLOGIST JOHN MENDALSON and yog guru swami RAMDEV have discussed regarding this and work has been startred in this direction. They told that AMERICA'S famous ANDERSON CANCER CENTRE 'S M.D. DR. JOHN MENDOLSON is interested to include yog and ayurveda for the prevention and cure of cancer.According to mr. TIJAREWALA,in this concern,ANDERSON CANCER CENTRE and patanjali yogpeeth will sign a MOU.In this study apart from dhyan and pranayam,herbal and ayurvedic medicines will also be studied in prevention and cure of ayurveda.
